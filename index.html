Monocle Schmonocle

Reality Augmenting Monocle Device Concept

‹ ›

Google Glass looks a little crass. You know those hands free things? That some people wear hanging off their ears? The wearer looks silly. Google Glass will make people look silly. This is a proposal to design the silliness into the object. Not many people wear monocles anymore. They would like to though. Because monocles are a little bit silly. And silliness is fun. Monocle plus augmented reality is ok.

This is a wee concept for a reality augmenting device. A monocle sits daintily in your pocket like you're some sort of early 20th century dandy. Everyonce in a while you pop it out to augment your reality. The idea is that a monocle is more fun than google glass which looks a bit crap, asks the wearer to keep it on for extended periods of time and scares people with its newness and supposed desire to steal their privacy.
